TechStream Bot — Privacy Policy
Last updated: 22 June 2026

1. Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how TechStream Bot ("the Bot", "we")
handles data when used by its Operator. Contact: azad.showlatov@gmail.com.

2. Information we access
When authorized by the Operator, the Bot accesses the following data
from TikTok via official APIs:
- OAuth access_token and refresh_token, used to publish videos on behalf
  of the authenticated account.
- Basic account profile (user id, username) for verification purposes.

3. Information we do not collect
The Bot does NOT collect, store, or process:
- Data about other TikTok users.
- Comments, messages, or interactions on published content.
- Any personal data beyond what is strictly required to publish content.

4. How we use information
OAuth credentials are used solely to publish content authorized by the
Operator to the Operator's own TikTok account. Credentials are stored
encrypted on the Operator's private server and are never shared with
third parties.

5. Data retention
Credentials are retained until revoked or rotated. The Operator may
delete tokens at any time, which immediately disables Bot publishing.

6. Third parties
We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

7. Children's privacy
The Bot is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly
process their data.

8. Changes
We may update this Policy. The "Last updated" date above will reflect
the most recent change.

9. Contact
For questions: azad.showlatov@gmail.com
